About us

Since 2009 Yourownmood (Steven and Gert-Jan) has worked for some of the most interesting projects and brands in the Netherlands. Started years ago with a broader view of “digital world” followed by the last years on a few specific disciplines. Delivering a high standard in online User Experience and Brand presence for applications, corporate, campaign and web-shops.

We strongly believe that the most successful projects are the ones were a great team is put together. Totally different people, with a different skillset, put together but capable of achieving the end goal with success. In short our work can be described as:

  • Contribute in an agile (scrum) project, start small from the very first beginning or work in larger teams.
  • Front-end development: Specialised in CSS development and architecture. Focusing on setting up a scalable, maintainable and good performing CSS architecture and workflow.
  • Visual- and Interaction Design: Translating a well known Brand into an online experience.
