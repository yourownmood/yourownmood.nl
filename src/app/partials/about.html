About us

Since 2009 Yourownmood (Steven and Gert-Jan), has worked on successful projects for some of the most strong brands in the Netherlands.

Started years ago with a broader view of “digital world” and the last few years focused on our expertise in UX design and Front-end development to help companies transform in the challenging and fast-moving digital landscape. Delivering a high standard in online User Experience and Brand presence for applications, corporate, campaign and web-shops.

We strongly believe that the key to for a successful project is when great mindsets and diversity of skillsets are put together as a team.

From top to bottom, we put ideas into action by working agile, while we encourage the spirit of teamwork and collaboration across all disciplines.

In short our work can be described as:

  • Contribute in an agile (scrum) project, start small from the very start or work in larger longer running teams.
  • Front-end development: Specialised in CSS development and architecture. Focusing on setting up a scalable, maintainable and good performing CSS architecture and workflow.
  • Visual- and Interaction Design: Translating a well known Brand into an online experience.
